Hi 🖐

I'm Ahmed, a .NET backend developer and a Computer Science student.

I built Jamrah to help students and anyone who wants to stay focused and productive — but couldn't find a simple, beautiful, and completely free app that does the job without all the clutter. So I made one.

My dream is to turn this into a full cross-platform mobile app someday — it's on my roadmap! In the meantime, it's fully open source. If anyone wants to contribute or build on it, you'd be helping the whole community.

I'm a backend .NET dev by trade — frontend isn't really my lane. This was built with vibe coding! I put a lot of effort into keeping the design clean and giving you full control over everything: colors, sounds, buttons — you name it. I'm still actively developing it, making it simpler every day.

If you just want a Pomodoro timer — the app will be just that. If you want habit tracking — it'll be just that too. Everything is under your control. You choose what to use and how to use it, exactly the way you like. No distractions. No payments.

I'm constantly updating the app, fixing bugs, improving the design, and adding more features. Hope you enjoy it! 😄💪
